Welcome to 47th Cafe

About Us

At 47th Cafe, we believe in serving happiness with every cup. Established in 2020, our cafe blends cozy ambiance, handcrafted beverages, and artisanal treats to create a unique experience for every guest.

What Makes Us Special

  • Freshly brewed coffee & signature drinks
  • Home-style bakery items made daily
  • Chic and cozy interiors with local art
  • Pet-friendly outdoor seating
  • Free Wi-Fi and workspace-friendly tables

Our Journey

  1. 2020 – Opened our first cafe in Boring Road, Patna
  2. 2021 – Gained popularity with students and freelancers
  3. 2022 – Launched our own line of cold brews
  4. 2023 – Expanded to 3 more locations in Bihar
  5. 2024 – Introducing a cloud kitchen & mobile coffee cart
